The OG -- reinvents the neighborhood brunch-ery, combining timeless inspiration from classic Americana with inventive and playful modern flair.

At The OG, we believe that brunch is not just a meal but a cherished ritual that brings people together. Our warm and inviting ambiance sets the perfect stage for friends and families to connect over a delightful spread of gourmet offerings. From early risers seeking a hearty start to their day to late brunchers craving a satisfying mid-morning meal, we cater to every palate.

OUR MENU

Our menus feature familiar, elevated comforts including a variety of gourmet sandwiches, soups and all-day breakfast options alongside our eclectic take on classic brunch options like our Smothered Breakfast Burrito, Steak & Eggs and Brioche French Toast.

Unique and craveable curiosities include our signature Doughnut Slider Burger, a quirky collection of boozy milkshakes and a pie case offering a rotating selection of sweet treats.

THE OG

EXPERIENCE

Located within the Rally Hotel, across 20th Street from Coors Field, we offer a brunching experience for both the young and young at heart.

Our two-toned Terrazzo floor mirrors the South Platte River as it winds through Denver, leading you through the restaurant to an outdoor patio located within McGregor Square for a front row seat to the bustling plaza and expansive outdoor screen.

Whether you're looking to kick-start your day, unwind on the weekends, or simply relish the joys of a leisurely brunch, The OG is your ultimate destination. Join us in celebrating the art of brunch, where every plate tells a story and every guest becomes part of our "OG" family.

Come and discover why "The Original" holds a special place in the hearts of locals and visitors alike, as we continue to redefine the essence of brunch in the heart of Denver, Colorado.

Come take a bite out of Denver at The OG -- we'll see you soon!

EDWARD DE DECKE

General Manager

Born in Montelimar, France, de Decker moved to Maryland with his family at a young age. de Decker went back to France every summer to work with his grandfather, on his farm in Provence. During the evenings de Decker would spend time in the kitchen with his grandmother, watching and helping her cook large family dinners. In his early years, de Decker found an immense passion for cooking, food, and the hospitality industry.

At 16, de Decker got his first job shucking oysters at Black’s Bar & Kitchen, in Maryland, and doing any job he could get his hands on from hosting and bussing tables to working in the kitchen as a prep cook. Before graduating from Virginia Tech’s R.B. Pamplin College of Business with a Bachelor of Science degree in business, with an emphasis in hospitality & tourism management, de Decker interned at The Balmoral Hotel in Edinburgh, Scotland, where he gained experience working at the one Michelin star restaurant, Number One.

Prior to joining Sage Restaurant Concepts, de Decker was the Assistant General Manager and Beverage Manager at Old Major, where he curated the bar and cocktail program and wine list. de Decker has held multiple food and beverage leadership roles, including experience as an Assistant Restaurant Manager at The Ritz-Carlton Downtown Denver and JW Marriott Desert Springs Resort & Spa in Palm Desert, where he oversaw various dining outlets. During his time at JW Marriott Desert Springs Resort & Spa, de Decker was acknowledged with the Griffin Award for strongly representing the vision of the JW Marriott brand and culture.

de Decker joined the Sage Restaurant Concepts team in February 2021 as an Assistant General Manager for The Rally Hotel’s two signature culinary concepts, The Original and The Rally Bar. With more than ten years of experience in the hospitality & tourism industry and an affinity for exemplary service and leadership, he was quickly promoted to General Manager in August 2021. de Decker draws from all his past experiences to help mentor and train his team towards success.
